Welcome to GLEE ACADEMY

In the late 40's the existing schools in Dhangadhi could no longer provide satisfactory guideline about modern education for the rural students in this area so to avoid this condition GLEE ACADEMY was established in 2048B.S. With a view to provide genuine and quality education in the area which was not being provided the required quality of education without any proper guidelines of the existing competition in this world. Started from Nursery class to Three in 2048B.S the school upgraded slowly to class 9-10. The first badge appeared in the SLC examination 2056BS.

Even though the main aim of school is to ample facilities for active academic pursuit, sincere efforts have been made to infuse in the students a sense of honesty, loyalty, dignity of labour and pride in the national heritage, tradition and culture.

Media Rooms:

There are fifteen media rooms in the school,each is well equipped with an extensive array of equipment including smart TV, smart boards, internet connection and a variety of resources and additional equipment.

Interactive Smart Boards:

This school has fourteen boards. The smart board are extensively used by the students to make presentation.

Classroom:

There are about 44 classrooms with comfortable broad space and audio-video facility so that students study in a comfortable environment.

Computer Lab:

There are two computer labs comprises of 25 computers in each lab including internet and projector.

Science Laboratory:

Science lab accommodates more than 25 students at a time. It provides an open environment for students to experiment and do research work.

Play Ground:

The school has a large playground for students to swing, play, slide and other things.

Transportation:

The school provides safe and secured transportation facility to 1000 students. Four vehicles and ten transportation team member covers Dhangadhi to Attariya. A list of charges is avaliable with the school administrator.

Medical Care:

All students receive regular medical check-up. Whenever specialized medical aid is required, emergency transport is available to take a students to hospital.

Library:

We have libraries containing learning and teaching resources that are available for all students. The school libraries are also known as the knowledge support centre which provides variety of interactive teaching resources.

CCTV:

Each and every classroom is well equipped with CCTV which helps to observe activities of the students.

School Canteen:

The school canteen provides a food service during lunch and break times. It offers a range of healthy snacks and peverages. Hygienic and strict quality control of food are regarded as top priorities.

Filter Water:
